### Simple Example\
\
Suppose a customer fills out a form:\
\
**Day 1**\
- Customer: \\"I am interested in a plot in Hyderabad.\\"\
- Agent explains details.\
\
If the customer doesn't buy immediately, the agent should contact them again.\
\
**Day 3 (Follow-up #1)**\
- Agent calls and asks:\
  - \\"Did you check the plot details?\\"\
  - \\"Do you have any questions?\\"\
\
**Day 7 (Follow-up #2)**\
- Agent sends a WhatsApp message:\
  - \\"We have a special offer this week.\\"\
\
**Day 15 (Follow-up #3)**\
- Agent schedules a site visit.\
\
These repeated contacts are called **follow-ups**.\
\

### Why are follow-ups important?\
\
Many customers don't buy on the first day because they:\
- Need time to think\
- Compare other options\
- Discuss with family\
- Arrange money\
\
Without follow-ups, the company may lose potential customers.\

What is this project?\
\
This project is a **Lead Scoring Dashboard** for a real estate company. Its main purpose is to help the sales team identify which customers (leads) are most likely to buy a property and should be contacted first. fileciteturn0file0\
\
Normally, companies receive many enquiries through calls, WhatsApp, websites, and forms. Managing all these customers manually is difficult and can cause missed sales opportunities. This dashboard solves that problem by automatically scoring leads as:\
\
- 🔥 **Hot Lead** – Ready to buy soon\
- 🌤️ **Warm Lead** – Interested but needs follow-up\
- ❄️ **Cold Lead** – Low chance of buying right now\
\
The score is calculated using:\
- Budget\
- Urgency\
- Questions asked\
- Site visit interest\
- Buying timeline fileciteturn0file0\
